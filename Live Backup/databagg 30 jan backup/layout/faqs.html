FAQ's

1. What is DataBagg?

DataBagg is an exciting Cloud based storage service, wherein, you need to upload your digital data once – and access it anywhere, anytime! We truly live-up to our slogan, which is, "Your Data In Your Bag".

DataBagg can be described as an application of Cloud computing.

Once your digital world is uploaded to DataBagg, it can be accessed via your mobile, tablet, PC or laptop. You can be assured that your information is completely safe and secured with us.

2. How to kick-start my Cloud journey with DataBagg?

Starting with DataBagg is super easy! All you need to do is, download our user friendly application on your desktop/laptop or mobile. Once you download our application, you can upload, download, access and share your data from any device, anytime and anywhere.

After your digital data is uploaded on our Cloud, it becomes extremely fluid and flexible as it is not platform dependent. You can start writing a blog on your PC, and finish it off on your smart phone and then publish it using your tablet.

3. What is Databagg USP – Unique Selling Proposition

There are many - But the best feature which we are really proud of is the auto-backup feature. It provides complete safety and security for whatever you are creating on our platform.

With auto-back feature, your data is saved as soon as it is entered on DataBagg.

Another feature is the auto-sync. Once your data is stored on our cloud, it is synchronized across all the devices which are connected via Databagg. There is no requirement to press ctrl-s every time.

With DataBagg, your data would be saved and synced even while you are sleeping!

4. Is my data safe with DataBagg?

We are very serious about safety of your data. We understand that information is today's asset – at no cost can we allow your data to be lost or corrupted.

DataBagg is a Cloud storage platform secured by 6 layers of security mechanism. We have implemented the most secured form of encryption methodologies to make sure your data remain safe with us.

5. Who are the ideal users of DataBagg?

Every person who uses Internet and saves digital information is an ideal user of DataBagg. Students can surf Internet at school/college and then upload the same via DataBagg. Once uploaded, it can be accessed at your home, friend's house or even coffee shop.

Corporate employees can work on spreadsheets in their offices, and then upload the same using DataBagg. Once uploaded, they can work on it while going home on their smart phones, and then again access it in their laptop once they have reached home.

Entrepreneurs, artists, musicians, gamers, dancers: Databagg is for everyone!

It is global access and auto-sync of your data, only with DataBagg.
